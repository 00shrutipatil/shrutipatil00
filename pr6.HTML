 Bhujbal Knowledge City
MET Bhujbal Knowledge City (MET BKC) in Adgaon,
Nashik, is a large educational
campus established in 2006, offering
programs in Engineering, Management, Pharmacy, 
Architecture, IT, and Science/Commerce,
affiliated with SPPU, and known for placements
in companies like Infosys, with facilities for
engineering, management, and other 
fields on a green campus.  

Bhujbal Knowledge City (BKC) has information
                  policies covering data privacy (protecting user
                  info via cookies, usage data), Right to
                  Information (RTI Act compliance with designated
                  officers), and various institute-specific rules 
                  like student conduct, research, and procurement,
                  all aimed at transparency, service quality, and 
                  legal adherence, accessible through their website
                  s for different faculties.
