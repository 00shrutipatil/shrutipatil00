The natural environment encompasses all living organisms 

    
The natural environment encompasses all living organisms 
        (plants, animals, microbes) and non-living components (air, water,
        soil, landforms, climate) that exist on Earth without significant 
        human interference, forming interconnected ecosystems that provide
        essential resources like clean air, water, food, and pollination,
        supporting human life and well-being but facing threats from
        pollution and climate change Key
